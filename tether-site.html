FAQ

Common questions.

Is the app really free for my employees? ▼
Each plan includes employee seats up to the plan limit — Crew includes up to 5, Enterprise up to 10. Your crew downloads the Tether app for free; you're billed based on your plan tier. The 90-day free trial includes full access with up to 2 linked employees.
What phones does Tether work on? ▼
Tether is currently available on Android. iOS support is in development. Both admin and employee apps are available on the Google Play Store.
How does Radar scheduling work? ▼
Radar shows a week or month view of your crew's scheduled jobs. You can see who's assigned to what, spot scheduling gaps, and add new assignments directly from the calendar view.
Can I export timecard data for payroll? ▼
Yes. Time entries are stored per employee per job and can be exported directly from the Timecard screen as Excel (.xlsx), CSV, or PDF — ready for payroll processing.
What trades is Tether designed for? ▼
Tether works for any business that manages a mobile field crew — general contractors, electricians, plumbers, landscapers, HVAC, painting, and more. If you're running people across job sites, Tether is built for you.
